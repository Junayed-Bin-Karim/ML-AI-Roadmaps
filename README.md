# ML & AI Roadmaps

Welcome to the **ML & AI Roadmaps** repository! This project provides structured learning paths for various AI and ML career roles, from beginner to advanced. Each role has its own roadmap detailing required skills, recommended courses, resources, and projects to build expertise.

---

##  Roles & Roadmaps

- **AI Agent Developer**: Build autonomous AI systems and multi-agent workflows.  
- **AI Engineer**: Design and implement AI solutions across domains.  
- **Computer Vision Engineer**: Specialize in image and video processing applications.  
- **Data Analyst**: Analyze and visualize data effectively.  
- **Data Engineer**: Build and maintain data pipelines and infrastructure.  
- **Data Scientist**: Develop predictive models and perform advanced analytics.  
- **Generative AI Engineer**: Work with generative models like GANs and LLMs.  
- **Machine Learning Engineer**: Build production-ready ML models and pipelines.  
- **NLP Engineer**: Specialize in Natural Language Processing applications.  
- **Software Engineering with ML-AI**: Integrate ML and AI into software development projects.  

---

## How to Use

1. Select the role you are interested in.  
2. Follow the suggested learning path and resources.  
3. Complete projects and exercises to reinforce your skills.
